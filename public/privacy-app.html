Privacy Policy — Lore

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

Lore is a web application that processes AI conversations locally in your browser to create structured handoffs. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data We Don't Collect

Lore does not:

  • Require accounts or sign-ups to use the app
  • Use analytics or tracking scripts
  • Use cookies
  • Send your personal data to our servers
  • Collect usage data of any kind

Data Storage

All data — including conversation logs, projects, settings, and API keys — is stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

API Keys

When you provide an API key, it is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. API requests are sent directly from your browser to the AI provider (Google AI, Anthropic, or OpenAI). We never see, intercept, or store your API keys on any server.

AI Processing

When you use Lore's AI features, your conversation text is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you selected for processing. We do not act as an intermediary. Please review each provider's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data.

Third-Party Services

Lore connects directly to the following AI providers when you use their services:

  • Google AI (Gemini)
  • Anthropic (Claude)
  • OpenAI

Waitlist

If you signed up for the Lore waitlist, your email address is stored in Supabase. You can request deletion of your email at any time by contacting us.
