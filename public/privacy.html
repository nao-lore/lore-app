Privacy Policy — Lore Capture

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

Lore Capture is a browser extension that extracts AI chat conversations from supported websites (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) and sends them to the Lore web application running in your browser.

Data Collection

Lore Capture does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All conversation data is processed entirely within your browser.

How It Works

  • When you click "Send to Lore", the extension reads the conversation from the current page.
  • The conversation data is passed directly to your Lore tab via a URL hash fragment.
  • No data is sent to any third-party server, analytics service, or remote endpoint.

Permissions

  • activeTab — To read the conversation from the current page when you click the extension.
  • scripting — To run the content extraction script on the page.
  • tabs — To find or open the Lore tab.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
