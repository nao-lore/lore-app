# Product Hunt — Competitive FAQ

For the PH FAQ section. Each comparison under 100 words.

---

**Q: How is Lore different from Claude Memory / ChatGPT Memory?**

Built-in memory remembers facts about you within one provider. Lore extracts structured project context — decisions, TODOs, blockers, resume checklists — and works across Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini simultaneously. It's cross-session, cross-provider project continuity, not personal memory. They're complementary: memory handles preferences, Lore handles project state.

---

**Q: How is Lore different from managing context manually in Notion?**

Lore auto-extracts structured context from raw conversations in 30 seconds. In Notion, you'd read through the conversation yourself, manually tag decisions and TODOs, and organize them into pages. Lore also tracks staleness, surfaces blockers, and lets you inject context back into your next AI session with one click.

---

**Q: Why not just re-explain my project each time?**

It works — until it doesn't. A 5-minute re-explanation misses nuance: why a decision was made, what was tried and rejected, which TODO was deprioritized and why. Lore captures that context automatically. Across multiple projects and providers, the time savings compound. Most users report saving 10-15 minutes per session.
