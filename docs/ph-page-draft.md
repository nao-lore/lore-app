## Description

**PH exclusive: 1 month Pro free (code: PHLORE2026)**

Lore turns your AI conversations into structured project context.

Paste a Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini conversation and get decisions, TODOs, blockers, and a resume checklist. Next session, inject it back with one click via the Chrome extension.

No signup. No server. Everything stays in your browser. Open source.

**How it works:**

1. Paste or import a conversation from any AI provider
2. Lore extracts structured context in ~30 seconds — decisions made, open TODOs, blockers, and exactly where to resume
3. Each snapshot feeds into a project dashboard that tracks your progress across sessions

Over time, you build a living record of everything you've done with AI — across providers, across projects. No more re-explaining. No more lost decisions.

**What makes it different:**

- Works with Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini — no provider lock-in
- Chrome extension injects context back into your next session with one click
- Runs entirely in your browser — no backend, no account required
- PWA with offline support — works without internet
- 1,258 tests, open source, fully auditable
- 8 languages supported

**Free:** 20 transforms/day, 3 projects
**Pro:** $12/mo ($10/mo annual) — unlimited everything
